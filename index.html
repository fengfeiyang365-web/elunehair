Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wigs, materials, care, and more.

To find your perfect fit, measure the circumference of your head from the front hairline, around the ears, to the nape of your neck. Most of our wigs come with adjustable straps to ensure a comfortable fit for head sizes 21.5" to 23.5".

Human hair wigs should be washed with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Gently comb with a wide-tooth comb, avoid rubbing or twisting. Allow to air dry on a wig stand. Style with heat tools up to 350°F. Store on a wig stand when not in use.

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery, provided the wig is in its original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached. Custom orders and clearance items are final sale.

The lifespan of your wig depends on the material and how often it's worn. Human hair wigs typically last 6-12 months with regular wear and proper care. Synthetic wigs generally last 3-6 months with regular wear.

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping times vary by destination, typically 7-14 business days for international orders. Tracking information is provided once your order ships.
